1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Desso ("Desso," "we," "our," or "us").

DESSO INC operates the Desso application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Desso is a social networking platform designed to help individuals discover and connect with other people nearby for friendship, networking, community engagement, and social interaction.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By creating an account, logging in, or using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

For questions, safety concerns, privacy requests, or moderation issues, contact:

support@desso.app

LOCATION INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------

Desso uses location information to provide core features of the Services.

We may collect:

• Precise GPS location
• Approximate location
• Location information while you are actively using the application

Location information is used to:

• Display aggregated heat maps showing general areas of user activity
• Facilitate local connections and discovery
• Calculate suggested meeting areas between matched users
• Improve matching and safety features
• Enable certain location-based functionality within the Services

Desso does not display a user's precise GPS location to other users.

Heat maps displayed within the application are aggregated and do not reveal any individual user's precise location.

Desso collects location information only when:

• You grant location permission; and
• You are actively using the application and have enabled location-based features, such as Open to Meet.

If you decline location permissions or disable location-based features, certain functionality of the Services may not be available.

Users may disable location permissions at any time through their device settings.
